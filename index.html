Featured Project: People Dashboard

An interactive dashboard providing comprehensive insights into human resources data and workforce analytics. The People Dashboard offers real-time visualization of key HR metrics and workforce dynamics, helping stakeholders make data-driven decisions about workforce management and organizational planning.

Dashboard Features

Workforce Distribution

Breakdown of headcount by nationality, division, and country, offering detailed views of workforce diversity and distribution across regions and departments.

Demographic Overview

Analysis of age and gender distribution by company and employment band, guiding initiatives to enhance gender diversity and address workforce planning.

Attrition Analysis

Data on involuntary and voluntary leavers, average tenure, and reasons for leaving, highlighting key turnover factors and retention challenges.

Hire Trends

Tracking of new hire trends by division, contract types, and work hour distribution, optimizing recruitment strategies and resource allocation.

Sick Summary

Comparison of sick days across companies, distinguishing between long-term and short-term leave, supporting health and wellness program development.
